About

Ladakh

Ladakh is a region in the northern Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir, known for its jagged mountain ranges, sparsely populated regions and Buddhist temples. It is bound by the Himalayas to the south and the Kunlun mountain range to the north.

Tibet

Tibet is home to the Tibetan people, located in the southwestern region of China. It's often considered "the Roof of the World" due to its unparalleled beauty and diverse natural surroundings. Once again, it is known for its Buddhist monasteries and the presence of Mt. Everest in the southern part of the region.

Europe

Budapest, the capital of Hungary, is famous for its monuments and relics that reflect its occupation by the Ottomans during the 17th century. Prague is one of the most beautiful cities in Europe, a historic city full of Medieval architecture and an abundance of UNESCO World Heritage sites. Paris, as we all know, is world famous for its rich history, tourist attractions, entertainment, fashion, and so on.
